My Project On The Effect of Sunlight on Plant Growth

One project I worked on involved conducting a simple science experiment to test how different amounts of sunlight affect plant growth. I planted several seeds in separate containers and placed them in areas with full sunlight, partial sunlight, and very little sunlight. Over a few weeks, I recorded the height and health of each plant, then compared the results to see how light exposure influenced growth. This project helped me understand the importance of careful observation, recording data, and drawing conclusions from experiments.
